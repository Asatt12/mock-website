Frequently Asked Questions

Are your supplements FDA approved?

Dietary supplements are not FDA-approved drugs. NorthPeak follows cGMP standards and uses third-party testing for quality and safety.

How long until I notice results?

Many customers report initial effects within 1-2 weeks, with more consistent outcomes in 4-8 weeks depending on routine and lifestyle factors.

Can I take these with my medications?

Consult your healthcare provider before starting any supplement, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or taking prescription medications.
