Yes, you can generally take Rhodiola and Ashwagandha together safely. These adaptogens are known for their complementary benefits—Rhodiola is often used for energizing focus, while Ashwagandha is popular for calming stress relief. When combined, they may enhance stress resilience, mood support, steady energy, and mental clarity, making them a powerful duo for overall wellness ([Healthline](https://www.healthline.com/health/rhodiola-and-ashwagandha-together), [PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12389708/)).

Both Rhodiola and Ashwagandha are well-tolerated at recommended doses, with minimal side effects such as mild digestive upset or dizziness. However, individual responses can vary, so it's wise to start with a lower dose and monitor how you feel. While no direct harmful interactions have been documented, it's important to be mindful of potential additive effects like dizziness from Rhodiola and sedation from Ashwagandha. If you are sensitive, consider trying each separately first ([ClinicalTrials](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT07461402)).
